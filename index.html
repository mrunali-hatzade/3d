The Origins

Born from a desire to elevate the everyday, Millet Bar began as a humble experiment in a dimly lit kitchen. We sought out the finest, ethically sourced cacao, understanding that true luxury respects its roots.

The Process

Time is our most valuable ingredient. We slow-roast our beans over 72 hours, coaxing out nuanced flavors that mass production destroys. The conching process takes days, ensuring a velvet melt that lingers on the palate.
